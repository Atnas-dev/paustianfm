CATS CM®
Arjen van Berkum, Strategic Wizard

CATS CM® is the recognized independent methodology for contract management. It’s holistic and agnostic approach is used by organizations managing procured and sold contracts. It has been developed for over 15 years and is currently in version 4. Large and small organizations in public and private sectors, ranging from hospitals, defense, energy, telcos, banks, insurers and many more, use this framework to create successful CM on their installed processes and procedures.

Hvad er CATS CM®

CATS CM® is the #1 contract management methodology worldwide. CATS stand for Contract Administration and Tracking Scenarios.

The method sets the standard on how contract management should be approached. It is designed for operational purposes, but also has a high strategic and tactical value, as it meets objectives on different levels and recognizes that the necessary human involvement should be primary over systems. It is a value driver for organizations, trainers and contract management professionals that want to become winners in contract management.
